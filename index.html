Privacy Policy (Basic – Android App) English Version Privacy Policy This application (“the App”) respects and protects the privacy of users. Information Collection and Use The App uses the device camera (android.permission.CAMERA) only for the purpose of taking photos as requested by the user. The App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data to any server or third party. Camera Permission Camera access is required solely to enable photo capture functionality within the App. Captured images are stored locally on the user’s device and are not uploaded or shared without the user’s explicit action. Data Sharing The App does not share any personal information with third parties. Data Security Since no personal data is collected or stored by the App, no personal data is processed or retained.
